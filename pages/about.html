About MyStayMate

Founded in India, MyStayMate is your trusted platform for finding and booking quality accommodations across the country. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, we help you discover comfortable and affordable stays—from hotels and guesthouses to homestays and boutique properties.

At MyStayMate, our mission is to simplify travel planning for every Indian traveler. We connect you with thousands of verified properties in popular cities, towns, and hidden gems across India, offering a seamless and secure booking experience.

Built by a team passionate about hospitality and technology, MyStayMate is committed to making domestic travel easier, smarter, and more reliable—without unnecessary fees or complications.

Why Choose MyStayMate

Wide Selection Across India

From budget stays in small towns to premium hotels in metro cities, we bring you a broad range of accommodations across India—all in one place.

Affordable Pricing

We work closely with property owners to bring you competitive pricing. No hidden fees—just transparent rates for every budget.

Instant Booking

Confirm your stay in just a few clicks. Our platform provides real-time availability and instant confirmation—so you can book with confidence.

No Booking Charges

We don’t charge extra booking fees. In most cases, you can also cancel or modify your reservation at no extra cost.

Safe & Secure Platform

Your data and payments are protected by robust encryption. We prioritize your privacy and safety throughout the booking journey.

Local Customer Support

Our India-based support team is available 24/7 to assist you—whether you're planning your trip or need help during your stay.
